French Breakfast Puffs

Ingredients

  • Margarine: 1/3 cup
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Egg: 1
  • Flour: 1 - 1/2 cups
  • Baking Powder: 1 - 1/2 cups
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Nutmeg: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Milk: 1/2 cup
  • Margarine, melted: 1/3 cup
  • Cinnamon: 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease muffin cups.
  3. Mix margarine, sugar, and eggs together.
  4. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and nutmeg together.
  5. Combine dry mixture and milk alternately with egg mixture.
  6. Fill each muffin cup 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes.
  8. Immediately roll in melted butter, then in mixture of cinnamon and sugar.
