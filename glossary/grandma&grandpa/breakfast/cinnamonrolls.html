Really Good Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • Yeast: 2 packages
  • Water (warm): 1/2 cup
  • Potatoes (mashed): 1 cup
  • Shortening: 2/3 cup
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Salt: 1 - 1/2 teaspoons
  • Eggs: 2
  • Milk (cooked until scalded): 1 cup
  • Flour: 6-8 cups
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Nuts (only if you're feeling nutty)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in water.
  2. Mix potatoes, shortening, sugar, salt, and eggs.
  3. Add milk and yeast mixture.
  4. Add flour.
  5. Knead: let rise 1 hour and punch down.
  6. Place in covered container in fridge. (Best to let sit in fridge overnight)
  7. Make dough into rolls.
    1. Roll dough into a square.
    2. Brush with butter, add cinnamon and nuts.
    3. Roll into log and cut in 1 inch slices.
    4. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-22 minutes.
