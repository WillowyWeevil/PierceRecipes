Chicken Enchilada Pie

Ingredients

  • Chicken, cooked, diced: 1/2
  • Onion (large), chopped, sauteed in margarine: 1
  • Cream of mushroom soup: 1 cup
  • Cream of chicken soup: 1 cup
  • Broth (beef or chicken): 1 cup
  • Chili Peppers (green): small can
  • Cheese, grated: 1/2 lb
  • Tortillas (corn): 1 package

Instructions

  1. Combine onions, soups, broth and peppers.
  2. Take half of the tortillas and place on the bottom of lightly greased casserole dish.
  3. Add layer of chicken.
  4. Pour half of soup mixture over chicken.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese.
  6. Repeat!
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
