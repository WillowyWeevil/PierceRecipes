Mexican Casserole

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: 1 lb
  • Onion: 1
  • Ranch style beans: 1 can
  • Cream of chicken soup: 2 cans
  • Chilis: 1 can
  • Enchilada sauce: As much as you want
  • Tomatoes: 1 can
  • Tortillas (corn): 1 package
  • Cheese!

Instructions

  1. Brown meat and onion in skillet.
  2. Add beans, soup, chilis to meat mixture.
  3. Layer tortillas and meat mixture.
  4. Pour tomatoes on top.
  5. Top with cheese.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees until bubbly.
