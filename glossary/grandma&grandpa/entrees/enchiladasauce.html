Enchilada Sauce

Ingredients

  • Flour: 8 tablespoons
  • Chili Powder: 8 teaspoons
  • Cumin: 4 teaspoons
  • Paprika: 1 teaspoon
  • Oregano: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Water: 4 cups

Instructions

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Mix with small amount of water to make thick paste.
  3. Continue adding water gradually, until thick and smooth.
