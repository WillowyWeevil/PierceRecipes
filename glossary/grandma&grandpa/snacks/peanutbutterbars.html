Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients

Bars

  • Butter: 1/2 cup
  • Peanut butter (crunchy): 1/2 cup
  • Brown sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Eggs: 2
  • Flour: 1 cup
  • Oats: 1 cup
  • Chocolate Chips: 3/4 cup

Topping

  • Peanut butter: 1/2 cup
  • Powdered sugar: 1 cup
  • Milk: 3-4 tablespoons

Instructions

Bars

  1. Combine all ingredients; mix well.
  2. Press mixture into 13 inch by 9 inch baking dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
  4. Once done cooking, sprinkle chocolate chips while still hot.

Topping

  1. Mix peanut butter, powdered sugar, and milk
  2. Pour over chocolate chips and spread!
