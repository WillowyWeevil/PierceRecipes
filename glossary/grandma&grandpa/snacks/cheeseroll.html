Our Cheese Roll

Ingredients

  • Velveeta: 1 lb.
  • Cream Cheese: 6 oz.
  • Garlic, smashed: 4 cloves
  • Pecans

Instructions

  1. Grind or mash all ingredients together.
  2. Roll in 2-inch diameter roll on wax paper.
  3. Added bonus! Sprinkle with chili powder if feeling risky
  4. Chill and slice
