Lemon Squares

(When life gives you lemons and you're tired of lemonade!)

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 cup
  • Powdered sugar: 1/2 cup
  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Salt: Pinch
  • Eggs: 4
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Flour: 6 tablespoons
  • Lemon juice: 6 tablespoons

Instructions

  1. Mix butter, powdered sugar, flour and salt.
  2. Pat in 13 inch by 9 inch pan or dish.
  3. Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes.
  4. Beat eggs.
  5. Add sugar, flour and juice, mix well.
  6. Pour over crust mixture.
  7. Bake at 325 degrees for 25 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar!
