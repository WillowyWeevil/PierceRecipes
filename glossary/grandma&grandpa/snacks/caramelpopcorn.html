Caramel Popcorn

Ingredients

  • Popped Popcorn: 8 quartz
  • Oleo: 2 sticks
  • Brown Sugar: 2 cups
  • Karo Syrup (white): 1/2 Cups
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: pinch
  • Mixed nuts: 1 can

Instructions

  1. Combine first four ingredients and boil for 5 minutes. Set off stove and add baking soda and salt.
  2. Pour over popcorn, mix well.
  3. Put in oven at 200 degrees for 1 hr. (Stir occasionally)
  4. Add mixed nuts
  5. Put in a bag to freeze or eat it fresh!
