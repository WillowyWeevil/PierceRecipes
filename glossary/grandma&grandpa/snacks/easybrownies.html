Easy Brownies

(So easy a kid could make it! Though I'm not sure... Have y'all seen kids nowadays?)

Ingredients

  • Butter, melted: 2 sticks
  • Cocoa: 3/4 cup
  • Sugar: 1 - 1/2 cups
  • Eggs: 4
  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Baking Soda: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Vanilla: 2 teaspoons

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add melted shortening, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Beat until smooth.
  4. Bake in greased and floured 10-1/2 inches by 15 inches pan at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
