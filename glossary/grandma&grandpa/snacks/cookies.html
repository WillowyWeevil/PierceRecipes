$250 Cookies

(Makes 112 cookies!)

Ingredients

  • Butter: 2 cups
  • Sugar: 2 cups
  • Brown Sugar: 2 cups
  • Eggs: 4
  • Vanilla: 2 teaspoons
  • Flour: 4 cups
  • Oatmeal, blended: 5 cups
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Baking Powder: 2 teaspoons
  • Baking Soda: 2 teaspoons
  • Chocolate chips: 24 oz.
  • Chocolate Bar, grated: 8 oz.
  • Nuts: 3 cups

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugars.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla. Set aside
  3. Mix dry ingredients.
  4. Combine with wet mixture, stir well.
  5. Add chocolate chips, grated chocolate bar and nuts.
  6. Roll in balls and place 2-inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 6-8 minutes at 375 degrees.
