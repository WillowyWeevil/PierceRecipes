Sausage Balls

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese, grated: 1 - 1/2 cup
  • Sausage (regular or spicy): 1 lb.
  • Bisquick: 3 cups

Instructions

  1. Allow sausage to warm slightly.
  2. Mix everything together.
  3. Form into 1 - 1/2 inch balls.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees. (If frozen bake for 30 minutes)
